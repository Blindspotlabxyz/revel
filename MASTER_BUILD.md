# Overview

Revel is an AI-powered product strategist that finds the hidden gaps stopping products from growing.

Instead of generating generic website audits, Revel reviews a product the way an experienced founder, product manager, UX researcher and strategist would.

Users simply paste a website.

Revel analyzes it.

Then generates an actionable roadmap.

The goal is simple.

Help builders see what they missed.

---

# Product

Name

Revel

Tagline

Short Description

Revel analyzes your website, product experience, messaging and competitors, then generates a prioritized roadmap your team can implement.

# Why Revel Exists

Most founders are too close to their own products.

Revel exists to bridge that gap.

# Success Criteria

A successful first version should allow a founder to:

Paste a website.

Understand what is wrong.

Know what to fix first.

Export a roadmap.

Complete the experience in under five minutes.

If that works consistently,

the MVP is successful.

# Brand Rules

Always say:

Find the hidden gaps stopping your product from growing.

Never say:

AI-powered next-generation revolutionary intelligence platform.

Avoid hype.

Earn trust through usefulness.

# Writing Rules

Use short sentences.

Avoid jargon.

Explain ideas simply.

Write for founders,

not engineers.

Every sentence should create clarity.

No unnecessary effects.
